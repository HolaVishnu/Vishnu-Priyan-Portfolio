Design Philosophy
"The portfolio is not a page.
It is a place you travel through —
where every stop has gravity."
Vishnu Priyan · @vpspaceman
01

Spatial over Flat

Every section lives in three dimensions. The camera is the cursor. Distance is the metaphor for depth of expertise.

02

Motion earns its cost

No animation exists for decoration. Each movement communicates narrative, physics, or state — never mere spectacle.

03

Performance is design

60fps is non-negotiable. The EffectComposer, AdaptiveDpr, and heavy shaders were all removed when they threatened the frame budget.

04

Silence speaks first

The void (#05060f) dominates. Light is earned — cyan and nebula only bloom where there is something worth illuminating.
